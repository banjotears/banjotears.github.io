MONSTER OF THE WEEK CAMPAIGN

Keeper | Scenario Writer

A coming-of-age action-horror campaign inspired by Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Gravity Falls, & Stranger Things

Players take on the role of a gang of teenagers in the fictional city of Culver, Washington, as they protect it from various
magical threats based on anything from urban legends to pop music.
Before each session, I would write the mystery, inspired by anything from horror media to songs I couldn't get out of my head.
During every session, I ensure each player was engaged, creating a space for collaborative storytelling surrounding themes of growing up,
queerness, and how identities are formed. By running a new mystery each session, players never know what they are up against, having to rely
